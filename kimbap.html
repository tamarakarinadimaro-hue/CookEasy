Korean Kimbap

Kimbap

Ingredients

  • Cooked rice
  • Seaweed sheets (nori)
  • Carrots, spinach, pickled radish
  • Eggs
  • Protein of choice (beef, tuna, or crab sticks)
  • Sesame oil & seeds

Instructions

  1. Season the cooked rice with sesame oil and salt.
  2. Prepare fillings: saut√© carrots, wilt spinach, slice radish, cook eggs into a thin omelet, and prep protein.
  3. Place a seaweed sheet on a bamboo mat, spread rice evenly, leaving space at the top.
  4. Layer fillings neatly in the center.
  5. Roll tightly with the mat, then slice into bite-sized pieces.
  6. Sprinkle with sesame seeds and serve!
