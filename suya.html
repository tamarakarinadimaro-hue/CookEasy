Delicious Nigerian Suya

Ingredients

  • 500g beef (thinly sliced)
  • 2 tbsp ground peanuts (or peanut butter powder)
  • 2 tbsp suya spice mix (yaji)
  • 1 tbsp ground chili pepper (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 medium onion (sliced into rings)
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Skewers (for grilling)

Steps

  1. Soak wooden skewers in water for 30 minutes to prevent burning.
  2. Mix ground peanuts, suya spice, chili pepper, paprika, and salt in a bowl.
  3. Rub the beef slices with oil, then coat them evenly with the spice mix.
  4. Thread the beef onto skewers.
  5. Grill over open flame, charcoal, or oven until cooked and slightly charred.
  6. Serve hot with sliced onions, fresh tomatoes, and cucumber.
