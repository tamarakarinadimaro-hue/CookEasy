Ramen Noodles Recipe

Ramen Noodles

Ingredients

  • Ramen noodles
  • Broth (chicken, pork, or veggie)
  • Soy sauce & miso paste
  • Toppings: boiled egg, sliced pork, seaweed, corn, green onions
  • Sesame oil & garlic

Instructions

  1. Cook ramen noodles according to package instructions.
  2. Prepare broth by simmering stock with soy sauce, miso, garlic, and sesame oil.
  3. Place noodles in a bowl and pour the hot broth over.
  4. Add toppings such as egg, pork slices, seaweed, and corn.
  5. Garnish with green onions and sesame seeds before serving.
