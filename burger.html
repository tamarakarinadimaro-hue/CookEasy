Juicy Burger

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 medium onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 egg
  • ½ cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce (optional)
  • 4 burger buns
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Sliced cheese (cheddar or American)
  • Ketchup, mayo, or mustard

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix ground beef, onion, garlic, egg, breadcrumbs, salt, pepper, and soy sauce until combined.
  2. Shape into 4 patties of equal size.
  3. Heat a pan or grill and cook patties for 4–5 minutes on each side (until fully cooked and juicy).
  4. Toast the burger buns lightly.
  5. Assemble: place lettuce, tomato, burger patty, cheese slice, and sauces inside the bun.
  6. Serve hot with fries or salad. Enjoy!
