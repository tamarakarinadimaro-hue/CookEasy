Nigerian Soups & Stews Recipes

Egusi Soup

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups ground egusi (melon seeds)
  • Palm oil
  • 2 cups spinach or ugu leaves
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 scotch bonnet peppers (blended)
  • Seasoning cubes and salt
  • Assorted meat and stockfish

Instructions:

  1. Heat palm oil and fry onions and pepper blend.
  2. Add ground egusi and stir fry for 5 minutes.
  3. Add meat, stock, and seasonings, then cook until thickened.
  4. Add spinach/ugu leaves and simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. Serve hot with pounded yam or eba.

Okra Soup

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups fresh okra (chopped)
  • Palm oil
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 scotch bonnet peppers (blended)
  • Seasoning cubes and salt
  • Assorted meat and fish
  • Spinach or ugu leaves

Instructions:

  1. Cook meat and fish in stock with seasonings.
  2. Add palm oil and pepper blend, simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Add chopped okra and stir until slimy and thickened.
  4. Mix in spinach/ugu leaves and simmer.
  5. Serve with fufu, eba, or pounded yam.

Pepper Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg goat meat, chicken, or fish
  • Pepper soup spice mix
  • 3 scotch bonnet peppers
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • Seasoning cubes and salt
  • Scent leaves (efirin)

Instructions:

  1. Cook meat or fish with onion, peppers, and seasoning.
  2. Add pepper soup spice mix and simmer for 20 minutes.
  3. Add scent leaves and cook for 5 minutes more.
  4. Serve hot with rice, yam, or agidi.

Vegetable Soup

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups ugu or spinach (chopped)
  • Palm oil
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 blended peppers
  • Seasoning cubes and salt
  • Assorted meat and fish

Instructions:

  1. Cook meat and fish with seasonings.
  2. Add palm oil, onion, and pepper blend, simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Mix in chopped vegetables and stir well.
  4. Cook for 10 minutes and serve hot with eba or pounded yam.

Ogbono Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 cup ground ogbono seeds
  • 1/2 cup palm oil
  • 2 cups assorted meat (beef, goat, tripe)
  • Stockfish or dry fish (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons ground crayfish
  • 2 scotch bonnet peppers
  • Spinach, bitterleaf, or ugu (to taste)
  • Salt and seasoning cubes (to taste)

Instructions

  1. Heat palm oil in a pot (do not bleach).
  2. Add ground ogbono and stir until it melts into the oil.
  3. Add meat stock and water gradually, stirring to avoid lumps.
  4. Add crayfish, pepper, meat, and fish. Simmer until cooked.
  5. Add vegetables last, simmer for 3 minutes, and serve with fufu.

Groundnut Soup

Ingredients

  • 2 cups roasted groundnut (blended into paste)
  • 1/2 cup palm oil
  • 2 cups meat or chicken
  • Stockfish or smoked fish
  • 2 tablespoons ground crayfish
  • 2 scotch bonnet peppers
  • Spinach or ugu leaves
  • Salt and seasoning cubes (to taste)

Instructions

  1. Blend roasted groundnut into a smooth paste.
  2. Heat palm oil, add blended paste, and stir for a few minutes.
  3. Add stock, meat, crayfish, and pepper. Stir well.
  4. Allow to cook until oil floats to the top.
  5. Add vegetables and cook for 5 minutes. Serve hot with rice or swallow.

Ewedu & Amala

Ingredients:

  • 1 bunch ewedu leaves
  • 1 tsp potash (kaun)
  • Pepper and seasoning
  • Ground crayfish
  • Amala flour (yam flour)

Instructions:

  1. Cook ewedu leaves with potash until soft.
  2. Blend or whisk into a smooth slimy texture.
  3. Add crayfish, pepper, and seasoning.
  4. Cook amala by stirring yam flour into boiling water until smooth.
  5. Serve ewedu with amala and stew on the side.
