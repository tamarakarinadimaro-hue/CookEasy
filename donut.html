Donuts

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Yeast
  • Glaze & sprinkles (for topping)

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, sugar, milk, eggs, butter, and yeast into a soft dough.
  2. Let the dough rise until doubled in size.
  3. Roll out and cut into donut shapes.
  4. Fry in hot oil until golden brown.
  5. Dip in glaze and add colorful sprinkles.
  6. Let set before serving.
