Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Food coloring (pastel pink, blue, purple)
  • Buttercream frosting (enough for 12 cupcakes)
  • Edible glitter, sprinkles, unicorn toppers (optional)
