Crispy Puff-Puff

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tsp dry yeast
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 1/4 cups warm water
  • Vegetable oil (for deep frying)

Steps

  1. Mix flour, sugar, yeast, nutmeg, and salt in a large bowl.
  2. Add warm water gradually and mix until smooth and stretchy.
  3. Cover and let the dough rise for 1 hour until doubled in size.
  4. Heat oil in a deep pan, scoop small portions of dough and drop into the oil.
  5. Fry until golden brown on all sides. Remove and drain on paper towels.
  6. Serve warm and enjoy your delicious Puff-Puff!
