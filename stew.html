Delicious Homemade Stew

Rich, hearty, and packed with flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg beef or chicken (cut into chunks)
  • 4 large tomatoes (blended or chopped)
  • 2 medium onions (chopped)
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2–3 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2–3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger (optional)
  • 2 seasoning cubes
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Fresh or dried pepper (to taste)

Steps

  1. Wash and season the meat with salt, seasoning cubes, onion, and spices. Cook until tender, then set aside (reserve the stock).
  2. Heat oil in a pot, add chopped onions and garlic, and fry until golden.
  3. Add the blended tomatoes and tomato paste. Fry on medium heat until the oil begins to separate from the sauce (about 10–15 minutes).
  4. Season with curry, thyme, pepper, and adjust salt to taste.
  5. Add the cooked meat and some of the stock into the tomato sauce. Stir well.
  6. Simmer for 10–15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the stew thickens and flavors blend nicely.
  7. Serve hot with rice, yam, or any dish of your choice.
