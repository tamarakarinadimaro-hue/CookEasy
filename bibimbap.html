Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked white rice
  • 1 small zucchini, julienned
  • 1 small carrot, julienned
  • 1 cup spinach, blanched
  • 4 shiitake mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 fried egg
  • Sesame oil and sesame seeds
  • 2 tbsp gochujang (Korean chili paste)
  • Soy sauce to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the rice and set aside.
  2. Saut√© zucchini, carrots, and mushrooms separately until tender.
  3. Blanch spinach, then squeeze excess water and season lightly with sesame oil.
  4. Place rice in a bowl, arrange all vegetables on top in sections, and add the fried egg at the center.
  5. Drizzle with sesame oil, sprinkle sesame seeds, and serve with gochujang.
  6. Mix well before eating and enjoy your colorful bibimbap! üåà
