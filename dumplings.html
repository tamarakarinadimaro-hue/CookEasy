Dumplings

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 200g ground pork (or chicken)
  • 1 cup cabbage, finely chopped
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • Pinch of salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Mix flour and water to form dough. Knead until smooth, then rest for 30 mins.
  2. In a bowl, combine pork, cabbage, green onions, garlic, soy sauce, sesame oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Roll dough thin and cut into small circles.
  4. Place filling in the center, fold in half, and pleat the edges to seal.
  5. Boil dumplings until they float, or pan-fry until crispy golden bottoms form.
