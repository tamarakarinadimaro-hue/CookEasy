Delicious Cookie Recipes 🍪

Double Chocolate Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 2 1/4 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 cup butter (softened)
  • 3/4 cup sugar + 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cream butter and sugars, add eggs.
  3. Stir in flour & baking soda, fold in chocolate chips.
  4. Bake 10–12 minutes until golden.

Peanut Butter Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 3 cups oats
  • 1 cup raisins
  • 1 tsp cinnamon

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cream butter and sugar, add eggs.
  3. Mix dry ingredients, fold in oats and raisins.
  4. Bake for 12 minutes.

Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda

    • Instructions:

    1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
    2. Cream peanut butter, butter, and sugar.
    3. Mix in egg, flour, and baking soda.
    4. Shape, press with fork, bake 10 minutes.

Oatmeal Raisin Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 2 3/4 cups flour
  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 tsp baking powder

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cream butter and sugar, add egg and vanilla.
  3. Mix flour and baking powder, form dough.
  4. Cut shapes, bake 8–10 minutes.

Sugar Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups flour
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 cups chocolate chunks

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cream butter and sugar, add eggs.
  3. Mix in flour and cocoa, fold in chunks.
  4. Bake 10–12 minutes.
