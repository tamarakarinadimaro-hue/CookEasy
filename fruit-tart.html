Ingredients

  • 1 pre-baked tart shell
  • 1 cup pastry cream (custard)
  • Strawberries, blueberries, kiwi, mango (or any fruits of choice)
  • 2 tbsp apricot jam (for glaze)
  • 1 tbsp water

Instructions

  1. Spread the pastry cream evenly inside the tart shell.
  2. Arrange sliced fruits beautifully on top of the cream.
  3. In a small pan, heat apricot jam with water to make a glaze.
  4. Brush the glaze gently over the fruits to give a shiny look.
  5. Chill for 30 minutes before serving. Slice & enjoy!
