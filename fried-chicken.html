Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken (cut into pieces) or 8 chicken drumsticks
  • 2 cups buttermilk (or plain yogurt + water mix)
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 2 teaspoons paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional, for spice)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Vegetable oil for deep frying

Steps

  1. Wash and pat the chicken dry with paper towels.
  2. Place the chicken in a bowl, pour in the buttermilk, season lightly with salt and pepper, and let marinate for at least 2 hours (overnight is best).
  3. In another bowl, mix flour, baking powder, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, cayenne pepper, salt, and black pepper.
  4. Heat vegetable oil in a deep pot or fryer to 175¬∞C (350¬∞F).
