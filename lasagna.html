Cheesy Lasagna

Layers of pasta, sauce, and melted cheese heaven

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 500g ground beef (or sausage)
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Boil the lasagna noodles until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a pan, heat olive oil and sauté onion and garlic until fragrant.
  4. Add ground beef, cook until browned, then stir in tomato sauce, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Spread a thin layer of sauce at the bottom of a baking dish.
  6. Layer noodles, sauce, ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used.
  7. Top with extra mozzarella and Parmesan for a cheesy crust.
  8. Bake uncovered for 30–40 minutes until golden and bubbly.
  9. Let cool for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
