Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • Pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a bowl.
  2. In another bowl, whisk milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients until just mixed.
  4. Heat a pan and pour ¬º cup batter for each pancake.
  5. Cook until bubbles form, flip, and cook until golden brown.
  6. Serve warm with syrup, fruit, or butter. Enjoy! üòã
