Mexican Tacos

Tacos

Ingredients

  • Soft or hard taco shells
  • Ground beef, chicken, or veggies
  • Shredded cheese
  • Lettuce & tomatoes
  • Salsa & sour cream
  • Cilantro & lime (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cook chosen filling (beef, chicken, or veggies) with seasoning.
  2. Warm taco shells in a pan or oven.
  3. Fill shells with cooked filling, cheese, and fresh toppings.
  4. Add salsa, sour cream, or hot sauce as desired.
  5. Garnish with cilantro and a squeeze of lime before serving.
