Delicious Shawarma

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts (sliced thin)
  • 1 large shawarma bread or tortilla wraps
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 2 tbsp ketchup
  • 1 tsp chili sauce (optional)
  • 1 small cabbage (shredded)
  • 2 carrots (grated)
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 2 cucumbers (sliced)
  • Spices: curry, thyme, garlic powder, salt, seasoning cube

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken with curry, thyme, garlic, salt, and seasoning cube. Grill or fry until golden.
  2. Mix mayonnaise, ketchup, and chili sauce for the creamy filling.
  3. Place shawarma bread on a flat surface, spread the sauce, then add chicken, cabbage, carrot, cucumber, and onion.
  4. Wrap tightly, then heat on a pan or sandwich maker for 2â€“3 minutes.
  5. Slice in half and serve warm. Enjoy your homemade Nigerian shawarma!
