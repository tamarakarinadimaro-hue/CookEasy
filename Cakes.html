Delicious Cakes Recipes 🍰

Chocolate Cake

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a cake pan.
  2. Mix dry ingredients (flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder).
  3. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla. Mix until smooth.
  4. Pour into pan and bake for 30–35 minutes.
  5. Let cool before serving.

Vanilla Cake

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup milk
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease the pan.
  2. Cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. Add eggs one at a time, then vanilla.
  4. Mix in flour, baking powder, salt, and milk alternately.
  5. Bake 25–30 minutes. Cool before slicing.

Red Velvet Cake

Ingredients:

  • 2 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp cocoa powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp red food coloring
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp vinegar

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease pans.
  2. Mix dry ingredients.
  3. Add wet ingredients: buttermilk, oil, eggs, food coloring, vanilla, vinegar.
  4. Pour into pans and bake 25–30 minutes.
  5. Cool and frost with cream cheese frosting.

Carrot Cake

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp cinnamon
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 cups grated carrots
  • 1/2 cup crushed pineapple
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease pan.
  2. Mix dry ingredients.
  3. Add eggs, oil, vanilla, carrots, and pineapple. Mix well.
  4. Pour into pan and bake 40–45 minutes.
  5. Cool and frost with cream cheese frosting if desired.

Lemon Cake

Ingredients:

  • 2 1/2 cups flour
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup milk
  • 3 eggs
  • Zest of 2 lemons
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease pan.
  2. Cream butter and sugar. Add eggs one at a time.
  3. Mix in flour, baking powder, salt alternately with milk.
  4. Add lemon zest and juice. Mix well.
  5. Bake 30–35 minutes. Cool before serving.
