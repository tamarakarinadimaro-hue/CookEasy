Sausage Rolls Recipe

Sausage Rolls

Ingredients

  • Puff pastry sheets
  • Sausages (pork, chicken, or beef)
  • 1 egg (for egg wash)
  • Salt & pepper
  • Optional: sesame seeds for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F).
  2. Cut puff pastry into rectangles and place a sausage on each.
  3. Roll the pastry over the sausage and seal the edges with a fork.
  4. Brush the tops with egg wash and sprinkle sesame seeds if desired.
  5. Bake for 20–25 minutes until golden brown and crispy.
  6. Let cool slightly, then serve warm!
