Crispy Yamarita

A crunchy, golden Nigerian yam delight üç†

Ingredients

  • 2 medium yams (peeled and sliced)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp ginger powder
  • 1 tsp paprika or chili powder
  • Salt and seasoning cube (to taste)
  • Vegetable oil (for frying)

Instructions

  1. Peel yam, cut into medium slices, and parboil for about 10 minutes until half-cooked.
  2. Drain the yam slices and let them cool slightly.
  3. In a bowl, whisk eggs with salt and seasoning.
  4. In another bowl, mix flour with garlic, ginger, and paprika.
  5. Dip each yam slice into the egg mixture, then coat with the seasoned flour.
  6. Heat oil in a pan and fry yam slices until golden brown and crispy on both sides.
  7. Drain on paper towels and serve hot with ketchup or pepper sauce.
